[Download Crate](https://crate.io/download/) and get started today! ## About Crate Crate is a new breed of database to serve today’s mammoth data needs. Based on the familiar [SQL syntax](https://crate.io/blog/sql-for-elasticsearch/), Crate combines high availability, resiliency, and scalability in a distributed design that allows you to query mountains of data in realtime, not batches.
