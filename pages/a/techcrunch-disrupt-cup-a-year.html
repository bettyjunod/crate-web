title: TechCrunch Disrupt Cup - A Year in the Life link: https://crate.io/a/techcrunch-disrupt-cup-a-year.html author: Jacob Fisher description: This December we returned to London to present the TechCrunch Cup back to the conference created: 2015/12/15 18:02:30 post_name: techcrunch-disrupt-cup-a-year status: publish post_type: post tags: techcrunch, crate, startups category: news We were thrilled to be invited to London to return the TechCrunch Disrupt Cup on the main stage. With thousands of attendees and hundreds of startups in attendance it was a great chance to check out the tech zeitgeist and all the latest innovations. We couldn’t have asked for a nicer audience - it’s been a year but the reaction from the number and enthusiasm of people afterwards told us that Crate is still solving an important problem. The TechCrunch battlefield is a great format that really encourages startups to pitch like they’ve never pitched before, and it was fantastic to see some of the startups competing this year - congratulations go to the eventual winner X! When we passed on the Cup we thought we made a video to show everyone what we got up to it for a year. We hope the winners will find as many good uses for it as we did!
