title: AVUXI processes billions of events from 65 million locations with Crate description: AVUXI aims to rank the popularity of every place on earth by using Crate created: 2015/07/30 11:00:00 post_name: avuxi-ranks-everywhere-on-earth-with-crate image: /media/1507/avuxi-logo.png author: Chris Ward casestudy_name: Alexis Battle casestudy_company: AVUXI casestudy_link: https://www.avuxi.com casestudy_industry: Location based marketing intelligence casestudy_location: Barcelona and London casestudy_usecase: Rapid and frequent processing of geolocation data highlight_1: Easy to import and export data highlight_2: Very easy to install and manage highlight_3: Fast Search highlight_4: Great Support post_type: post template: case-study.html category: casestudy AVUXI increases online travel revenues by ranking the popularity of every place on Earth and delivering customers a superior booking experience. The company collects geotagged data from over 65 million locations, processing over 20 million events every day, cleaning, matching and categorizing the data collected. Utilizing Crate alowed AVUXI to gather and process this data faster and more efficiently than any other solution they looked into and the [support for geolocation data](/docs/stable/sql/data_types.html?highlight=geo#geo-point-data-type) was a positive. AVUXI were using a 4 node Crate cluster with aproximately 800 million records when they discovered an issue with concurrent INSERT requests. Thanks to Crate's Open Source nature and swift response of the core team, the issue was fixed quickly in a hotfix release and all users benefited from the issue report. > "We were going crazy looking more information about how to fix this issue. We really appreciate your efforts and dedication in order to help us as fast as you did." > "It is a pleasure working with companies like yours, that listens and cares about their customers." AVUXI now have two Clusters of three nodes each and continue to recommend Crate to anyone they meet who needs to handle large data sets.
